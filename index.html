What is Neuro Cup Competition?

It is a neuroscience competition, in which high school students compete to test their knowledge of neuroscience!

The Certificates

Certificates of participation for all participants have over 60% in the Phase One Exam, whether participants in the first stage only for participants in the second stage as well.

Awards

There will be awards for first and second places on the final stage, those don't lucky enough to reach this places will receive Certificates of participation.

Playoffs

Only 64 will qualify for the second stage, and they will line up in pairs until they reach 32, and this will be repeated until only 2 remain.

The all stages for the final

Phase 1

It will include all participants, and it will be a test consisting of 50 questions in 60 minutes. To pass this test and obtain a certificate of participation, you must at least obtain a result of 60%. Only 64 people will qualify for the next stage, and they will be those who have obtained a highest 64 degrees.

Phase 2

Only those who qualify for the first stage will be able to enter, which will be 64 in number, and there will be a lottery for all contestants, which will be done randomly in pairs, and the elimination will be made between each of them through a test. Whoever gets the highest result will qualify for the next stage, Round of 32, and so on until We reach the final stage, which consists of only 2.
